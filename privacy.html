Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Workfern. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our products, applications, and services.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Our core philosophy is that your data belongs to you.

  • Local Processing: Whenever possible, our software processes your data locally on your device. We do not store, read, or transmit your personal content or usage habits to our servers.
  • Permissions: Our products only request the minimum device or browser permissions necessary to provide their core functionality.

3. Payment and Order Processing

We partner with an authorized third-party Merchant of Record (MoR) to securely handle all checkout processes, licensing, subscription management, and global tax compliance.

  • When you purchase a license or subscription, your transaction is processed securely by our designated MoR.
  • We do not collect, process, or store your credit card or sensitive payment information on our servers.
  • Our payment partner may collect your name, email address, and billing details to process the order. Please review their specific Privacy Policy provided during the checkout process for more details.

4. Website Analytics & Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to ensure the site functions properly and to track anonymous website traffic (such as page views and conversion rates) to help us improve our products.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@workfern.com.
