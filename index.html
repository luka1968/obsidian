Why do you need this plugin?

In subscription-based e-commerce businesses, "failed payments"—caused by issues such as expired credit cards, insufficient funds, or bank rejections—are one of the primary causes of lost profits. Subscriptions Payment Recovery for WooCommerce is an automated revenue-generating tool designed specifically for your WooCommerce store. Operating entirely in the background, it automatically detects failed subscription renewal orders and intelligently guides customers to update their payment methods. Requiring absolutely no manual intervention, the system works silently to recover valuable revenue that would otherwise have been lost.

Automated Recovery Emails

The plugin features a scientifically designed, built-in 3-step email drip sequence. When a payment fails, the system automatically sends carefully crafted recovery emails on Day 1, Day 3, and Day 5, directing customers directly to the payment update interface.

Recovery Analytics Dashboard

Provides professional visualizations and data dashboards that make recovery performance crystal clear. Track key metrics in real-time including total failed payments, recovery success rate, and total revenue recovered.

Full Recovery Log

Say goodbye to "black box" operations. The system provides detailed, paginated logs that record every failed payment attempt and its current status, allowing your support team to easily verify a customer's status at any time.

Minimalist Installation

No complex webhook configuration required. Unlike other recovery tools that require cumbersome external callbacks, this plugin integrates deeply and directly with WooCommerce for a truly "Plug & Play" experience.

Ultimate Gateway Compatibility

Supports any payment gateway that supports WooCommerce subscription failure events. Fully compatible with both the official WooCommerce Subscriptions and YITH WooCommerce Subscription plugins, plus full support for High-Performance Order Storage (HPOS).
