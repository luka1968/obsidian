Donald Trump

During his first presidency, Trump imposed a travel ban on seven Muslim-majority countries, expanded the Mexico–United States border wall, and enforced a family separation policy on the border. He rolled back environmental and business regulations, signed the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, and appointed three Supreme Court justices. He withdrew the U.S. from agreements on climate, trade, and Iran's nuclear program, and started a trade war with China. In response to the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, he downplayed its severity, contradicted health officials, and signed the CARES Act. After losing the 2020 presidential election to Joe Biden, Trump attempted to overturn the result, culminating in the January 6 Capitol attack in 2021. He was impeached in 2019 for abuse of power and obstruction of Congress, and in 2021 for incitement of insurrection; the Senate acquitted him both times.

In 2023, Trump was found liable in civil cases for sexual abuse and defamation and for business fraud. He was found guilty in 34 counts of falsifying business records in 2024, making him the first U.S. president convicted of a felony.

Core Features

Everything you need to supercharge your reading and research workflow.

Seamless Floating Toolbar

  • Frictionless Capture: Simply select any text on a webpage, and a floating toolbar will appear, allowing you to quickly highlight it in yellow.
  • Intuitive UI: No need to right-click or use complex keyboard shortcuts. The highlighting utility appears right where your cursor is.

Notes & Annotations

  • Contextualize Your Research: Go beyond simple highlights by adding personal notes and annotations directly to your selected text.
  • All-in-One View: Click the extension popup icon to view, manage, and read through all the highlights and notes you've collected on the current page.

Robust Re-hydration Algorithm

  • Survives Page Reloads: Uses the industry-standard W3C Text Quote Selector algorithm (Exact + Prefix + Suffix fingerprinting).
  • Built for Modern SPAs: Works flawlessly on dynamic, single-page apps like Twitter, YouTube, and complex news sites.

Effortless Obsidian Integration

  • One-Click Markdown Export: Click "Export to Obsidian" to instantly compile page highlights and notes into clean Markdown.
  • Streamlined Knowledge Management: Pre-formatted structure, allowing you to easily paste or save it to build your second brain.

Local-First, Privacy-Centric Architecture

  • Persistent Local Storage: All highlights and notes are saved entirely on your local machine using persistent local storage.
  • Keep Your Data Yours: Operating on a local-first philosophy, there is no required cloud syncing, ensuring your reading habits and data stay completely private and fast.
